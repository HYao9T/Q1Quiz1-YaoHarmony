Monsters, INC.

Monsters, inc. is an animated comedy film that takes place in the city of "Monstropolis" where, they scare children for their screams in order to power their city. Our main characters are Sulley and Mike Wazowski,
who were enemies at first but as the story progresses they become best friends and protect a child named "Boo" to bring her safely back to the human world. The main idea of this movie is the power that teamwork brings and
the joy of finding and achieving your dreams.
